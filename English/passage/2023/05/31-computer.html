# Computers

Computers are a vital part of modern life. They have revolutionized the way we work, communicate, and access information.

translation

sentence:

计算机是现代生活的重要组成部分。它们改变了我们工作、交流和获取信息的方式。

words:

vital
美: [ˈvaɪt(ə)l] 英: ['vaɪt(ə)l]

adj. 必不可少的；对…极重要的；生命的；维持生命所必需的

revolutionize
美: [ˌrevəˈluʃ(ə)nˌaɪz] 英: [ˌrevəˈluːʃəˌnaɪz]

v. 革新；使革命化；引起革命；鼓吹革命思想

access
美: [ˈækˌses] 英: [ˈækses]

v. 进入；〈正式〉到达；【IT】获得或储存(电脑文件)
n. 通道；入口；机会；权力

One of the most important functions of computers is processing data. This can include tasks such as word processing, spreadsheet editing, and graphic design. With the help of powerful processors and high-performance software, computers can handle large amounts of data quickly and efficiently.

translation

sentence:

计算机最重要的功能之一是处理数据。这可以包括诸如文字处理、电子表格编辑和图形设计等任务。在强大的处理器和高性能软件的帮助下，计算机可以快速而高效地处理大量数据。

spreadsheet
美: [ˈspredˌʃɪt] 英: [ˈspredˌʃiːt]

n. 图形
　adj. 绘画的；书画的；图样的；图案的

美: [ˈprɑˌsesər] 英: [ˈprəʊˌsesə(r)]

n. (数据等的)分理者；【自】信息处理机；〈美〉农产品加工者

performance
美: [pəˈfɔrməns] 英: [pə(r)ˈfɔː(r)məns]

n. 性能；表现；业绩；表演

英: [ɪ'fɪʃ(ə)ntli]

Another important aspect of computers is their ability to connect people from all over the world. Through the internet, we can communicate with others in real-time, no matter where they are located. This has transformed the way we do business, learn, and socialize.

translation

sentence:

计算机的另一个重要方面是它们能够连接来自世界各地的人们。通过互联网，我们可以实时与他人沟通，无论他们身在何处。这已经改变了我们做生意、学习和社交的方式。

words:

aspect
美: [ˈæspekt] 英: ['æspekt]

n. 方面；层面；外观；方位

美: [ˈsoʊʃ(ə)lˌaɪz] 英: [ˈsəʊʃəlaɪz]

v. 交际；使适应社会；使社会主义化；按社会主义原则行事

Computers have also had a significant impact on education. With online learning platforms and educational software, students can access educational resources from anywhere at any time. This has made education more accessible and convenient for people of all ages and backgrounds.

translation

sentence:

计算机对教育也产生了重大影响。通过在线学习平台和教育软件，学生可以从任何地方随时访问教育资源。这使得教育对于各个年龄和背景的人来说更加易于获得和方便。

significant

Overall, computers have become an essential tool in our daily lives. Whether we are working, communicating, learning, or simply browsing the internet, computers play a critical role in making these activities possible. As technology continues to advance, it is exciting to think about the new ways that computers will continue to shape our world.

translation

sentence:

总的来说，计算机已经成为我们日常生活中必不可少的工具。无论我们是工作、交流、学习还是只是浏览互联网，计算机都在使这些活动成为可能的关键作用。随着技术的不断进步，我们很兴奋地想象计算机将继续以新的方式塑造我们的世界。
