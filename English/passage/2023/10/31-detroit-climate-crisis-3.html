# Detroit's Climate Crisis — and How to Build a Resilient Future Everywhere (3)

About two years ago, eight inches of rain fell over Detroit over a period of three days.

And not just Detroit, but southeast Michigan.

Scientists describe this as a more-than-500-year rain event.

Now, eight inches of rain doesn't seem like a lot of rain, but this one rain event flooded our streets.

Basements became filled with storm water and sewage.

The repair costs were insurmountable for many families,

and with nearly half of Detroiters renting, it often led to homelessness.

Climate change exacerbates and compounds economic inequity in our city.

translation

sentence:
大约两年前，底特律三天内降雨量达到八英寸。
不仅是底特律，还有密歇根州东南部。
科学家将此描述为一场长达 500 多年的降雨事件。
现在，八英寸的降雨看起来并不是很多，但这一场降雨淹没了我们的街道。
地下室充满了雨水和污水。
对于许多家庭来说，维修费用是难以承受的，
而且由于近一半的底特律人租房，这常常导致无家可归。
气候变化加剧并恶化了我们城市的经济不平等。

phrase:
fell over -> 落在：指雨水落在某个地方。 / 摔倒：失去平衡并跌倒的动作。
a period of -> 一段时间：指一段时间内发生的事情。
storm water -> 暴雨水：源自降水的水，包括大雨和冰雹、雪水的融化水。

But I believe there are three things that we can do to begin to change the resilient future for Detroit.

First, we have to begin to create an economic environment in which all Detroiters can grow wealth.

Advanced technology and tech-based businesses are the fastest-growing industries in Detroit, but also the nation.

It's these businesses that will contribute to solving our climate dilemma and make millions, even billions, doing it.

But unless we include Black and brown Detroiters, both as workers and investing in founders, we will leave our largest demographic out of our future.

Second, we have to stop only responding when there's a crisis situation, like flooding and air quality control, and begin to think about green stormwater infrastructure and solar and renewable energy proactively.

translation

sentence:
但我相信我们可以做三件事来开始改变底特律的弹性未来。
首先，我们必须开始创造一个让所有底特律人都能增长财富的经济环境。
先进技术和科技企业是底特律乃至全国增长最快的产业。
正是这些企业将为解决我们的气候困境做出贡献，并通过这样做赚取数百万甚至数十亿美元。
但除非我们将黑人和棕色人种底特律人纳入其中，无论是作为工人还是对创始人进行投资，否则我们将把我们最大的人口排除在我们的未来之外。
其次，我们必须停止只在出现洪水和空气质量控制等危机情况时才做出反应，并开始积极考虑绿色雨水基础设施以及太阳能和可再生能源。
