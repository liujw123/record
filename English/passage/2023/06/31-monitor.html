# monitor

A computer monitor is an electronic device used to display images and text on a computer screen. It is an essential component of a computer system, as it allows users to interact with software applications and access digital content. Monitors come in various sizes and types, including LCD, LED, OLED, and CRT monitors.

translation

计算机显示器是一种用于在计算机屏幕上显示图像和文本的电子设备。它是计算机系统的重要组成部分，因为它允许用户与软件应用程序互动并访问数字内容。显示器有各种尺寸和类型，包括 LCD、 LED、 OLED 和 CRT 显示器。

The earliest monitors were CRT (Cathode Ray Tube) monitors, which used cathode rays to project images onto a phosphor screen. These monitors were bulky and heavy, and their image quality was not as good as modern monitors. In the 1980s, VGA (Video Graphics Array) monitors became popular, offering higher resolution and better color accuracy than CRT monitors.

translation

最早的显示器是 CRT (阴极射线管)显示器，它利用阴极射线将图像投射到荧光屏上。这些显示器又大又重，其图像质量不如现代显示器。在20世纪80年代，VGA (视频图形阵列)显示器变得流行起来，比 CRT 显示器提供更高的分辨率和更好的色彩准确度。
