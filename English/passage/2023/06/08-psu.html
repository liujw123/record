# PSU

A computer power supply unit (PSU) is an essential component of any computer system. It provides the necessary electrical energy to power the various components of the computer, including the central processing unit (CPU), memory, hard drive(s), graphics card(s), and other peripheral devices.

words

peripheral
美: [pəˈrɪf(ə)rəl] 英: [pə'rɪf(ə)rəl]

n. 外部设备；计算机外围设备；附加设备；辅助设备
adj. 周围的；外围(的)；外设的；外表面的
网络 外围的；周边的；周边设备

translation

计算机电源单元(PSU)是任何计算机系统的重要组成部分。它提供必要的电能来驱动计算机的各种部件，包括中央处理器(CPU)、存储器、硬盘驱动器、显卡和其他外围设备。

The PSU converts the alternating current (AC) power supplied by the wall outlet into direct current (DC) electricity that can be used by the computer's internal components. It also regulates the voltage and current delivered to each component to ensure that they receive the proper level of power to operate efficiently and safely.

电源模块将墙壁插座提供的交流电(AC)转换为计算机内部组件可以使用的直流电(DC)。它还调节输送到每个组件的电压和电流，以确保它们获得适当的功率水平，以有效和安全地运行。

There are two main types of PSUs: linear and switched-mode. Linear PSUs use transformers to step down AC power to DC voltage, while switched-mode PSUs use high-frequency switching techniques to convert AC to DC with greater efficiency and lower noise levels.

有两种主要类型的电源模块:线性和开关模式。线性电源模块使用变压器将交流电源降压为直流电压，而开关模式电源模块使用高频开关技术将交流转换为直流，效率更高，噪音更低。

  • step down -> 走下；退出；【电】下降；辞退

When selecting a PSU for a computer system, it is important to consider factors such as the total wattage required by the system, the efficiency of the PSU, its size and weight, and its compatibility with the specific motherboard and other components being used. Additionally, it is important to ensure that the PSU meets any certification or regulatory standards set by organizations such as the ENERGY STAR program in the United States.

Overheating is a common problem with PSUs, and it can cause damage to the components and even result in fire. To prevent overheating, it is important to ensure that the PSU is properly installed and that there is sufficient airflow around it. Regular maintenance, such as cleaning the fans and checking the connections, can also help prevent overheating.
