# GPU

A GPU, or graphics processing unit, is a type of computer hardware designed to accelerate graphical processing. It was originally developed for use in gaming computers, but has since been adopted by other industries such as video editing and 3D modeling.

translation

GPU是计算机硬件中的一种，专门设计用于加速图形处理。它最初是为游戏电脑开发的，但已经被其他行业如视频编辑和3D建模所采用。
